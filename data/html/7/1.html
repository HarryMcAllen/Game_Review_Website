Become The Best

Apex Legends is a battle royale game that adds it own twists by adding charecters that have their own special abilities, similar to Rainbow Six Seige or Overwatch. With teams consisting of 3 players and only one charecter per team it adds tactical depth to the game which becomes more and more importanted the better you become. It also has a extensive ranking system, giving a reason for players to keep coming back to improve achieve the next rank, with season resets meaning that you have to prove yourself each season.
