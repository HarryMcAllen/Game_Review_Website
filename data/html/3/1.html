Defining A Genre

Since Fortnite has been released it quickly became the leading battle royale game and has set they way for all games of all genres coming after. It has created a large cultural impact as most people with internet access are aware of Fortnite, no matter their age, something that can't really be said for many other games. While it does appeal to a younger audience with its playstyle and cartoon artwork, the effect it has had on the gaming industry cant be underestimanted.
