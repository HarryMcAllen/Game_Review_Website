Thrilling Gameplay

NBA 2k22 brings all the fun of a professional basketball game to your home through your Playstation 5, it provides an fun way to ease yourself into professional basketball by learning team and player through the game the user can be introduced to professional basketball and reconsise franchises from the game. On top of this it also provides intense gameplay as you create your myplayer and try to become the greatest basketball player of all time, carrying your team to championships or you can test your skills against other players online.
