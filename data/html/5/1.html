Best You Can Get

Assetto Corsa Competizione is a racing simulator based upon the SRO GT3 series, offering the user all the GT3, GT4 and cup cars available in the series while also providing all of the tracks SRO races at. Tracks consisting of Silverstone, Spa-Francorchamps, Brands Hatch and more. It provides a simulation of driving a GT3 as realistic as most people will ever be able to expiernce with real life acurate physics of the cars weight shifting through differnt actions, realistic tyre model which accounts for slip angle and car setup which realisticaly affects the cars handling. In terms of real racing sims on console it goes unrivaled.
