Become an F1 Driver

The F1 series always provided a fun and challenging experience that is driving an Formula 1 car, it continues its career mode with "my team" where you enter the F1 grid as an 11th team have to upgrade your car from being one of the worse on the grid to one that can challenge for championships. It caters to all audiences with its assists feature, alllowing players to adjust the difficulty of driving the car by turing of features such as traction control, abs or racing line to better simulate the feeling of being an F1 driver. It also provides a online sytem allowing users to test their skills in multiplayer lobbies against other players.
