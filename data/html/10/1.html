Continuing The Series

The Farming Simulator series has a long history of games that provide the user with endless hours of fun through various activities. It allows the player to follow what ever agricultural career path they want, from contracting for local farmers, growing and harvest your own crop, forestry or a combination of them all. Farming Simulator has something for everyone, with a wide range of machinery at different price points it allows the user to start small and build their way up to owning lots of machinery and land. This in combination with the active modding community it means that countless hours of fun can be had while playing Farming sim.
